Frequently Asked Questions

Does StorySplitter AI support multi-page sheets?

Yes. You can upload multiple sheets at once. They will appear as a structured sequence in the sidebar where you can cycle through pages and add new ones anytime.

Can I rename individual panels?

Absolutely. You can edit panel labels directly inside the canvas by double-clicking on the label badge, or modify them in the right-hand panel list.

What image formats are supported?

We accept WebP, PNG, JPEG, and TIFF sheets. Export outputs can be selected as lossless PNG or high-quality JPEG compression.

Is it free to use?

Yes! StorySplitter AI is a free open-source utility designed to speed up the pre-visualization workflow for creators worldwide.
